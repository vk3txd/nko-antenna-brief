2026/06/04 - More testing. Built a 4:1 UnUn using a 2 stack of FT114-43 cores, then wound on 7 turns bifilar to make a 4:1 UnUn. Then I spent a few hours testing with my dummy load.
- FT114-43 is very similar in performance to LO1234 cores, only fractions of a percent diffrence
- I tested at 3.6MHz, loss increated, and at 14MHz where loss reduced.
- As a cheeck, I re-tested the LO1234 x 2 core rebuild and got the same kinds of loss figures.
- Overall, 7MHz is a "middle ground" for loss testing. At 3.6MHz is increases, and at 14MHz it reduces.
- FT114-43 cores and LO1234 cores responded simmilarly in terms of loss to frequency.
